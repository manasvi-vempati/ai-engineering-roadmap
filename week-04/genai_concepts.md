## 🧠 What Does a Neural Network Do?

A **neural network** is a machine learning model inspired by the human brain. It learns patterns from data and uses those patterns to make predictions or decisions.

### How It Works
1. Receives input data.
2. Processes the data through hidden layers.
3. Learns by adjusting weights during training.
4. Produces an output or prediction.

### Example
A neural network can learn to recognize handwritten digits (0–9) from images in the MNIST dataset.

### Common Applications
- Image recognition
- Speech recognition
- Fraud detection
- Medical diagnosis
- Recommendation systems

## 🤖 What Does an LLM Do?

An **LLM (Large Language Model)** is an AI model trained on massive amounts of text. It understands and generates human-like language.

### What an LLM Can Do
- Answer questions
- Summarize documents
- Translate languages
- Generate code
- Write emails, essays, and stories
- Explain complex concepts
- Extract information from text

### Examples of LLMs
- ChatGPT
- Google Gemini
- Claude
- Microsoft Copilot

## 💬 Why Do Prompts Affect Output Quality?

A **prompt** is the instruction given to an AI model. The quality of the prompt directly affects the quality of the response.

### Poor Prompt

Tell me about AI.

### Better Prompt

Explain Artificial Intelligence for beginners in 200 words. Include its definition, advantages, applications, and a real-world example.

### Why Better Prompts Work
- 🎯 Clearly define the task.
- 📝 Provide enough context.
- 📋 Specify the desired format.
- 👥 Mention the target audience.
- 📏 Include length or detail requirements.

Better prompts help the AI generate more accurate, relevant, and useful responses.

## 🔍 What Are Embeddings Used For?

**Embeddings** are numerical (vector) representations of words, sentences, images, or documents. They capture the meaning of data so that similar items are represented by similar vectors.

### Example

Cat  → [0.21, 0.45, 0.67, ...]
Dog  → [0.23, 0.44, 0.65, ...]
Car  → [0.91, 0.12, 0.08, ...]

Since **"Cat"** and **"Dog"** have similar meanings, their embeddings are closer together than those of **"Car"**.

### Uses of Embeddings
- 🔎 Semantic search
- 💬 Chatbots and question answering
- 📄 Document retrieval
- 🎯 Recommendation systems
- 😊 Sentiment analysis
- 🏷️ Text classification
- 🧩 Clustering similar documents
- 🤖 Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
